<p>Megalodon is a modified version of the <a href="https://github.com/mastodon/mastodon-android">official Mastodon Android app</a> adding important features that are missing in the official app, such as the federated timeline, unlisted posting, bookmarks and an image description viewer.</p>

<p><b>Key features</b></p>

<ul>
  <li>
    <b>Unlisted posting:</b>
    Post publicly without having your post show up in trends, hashtags or public timelines.
  </li>
  <li>
    <b>Federated timeline:</b>
    See all public posts from people on all other Fediverse neighborhoods your home instance is connected to.
  </li>
  <li>
    <b>Image description viewer:</b>
    Quickly check whether an image or video has an alt text attached to it.
  </li>
  <li>
    <b>Pinning posts:</b>
    Pin your most important posts to your profile and see what others have pinned using the “Pinned“ tab.
  </li>
  <li>
    <b>Bookmarks:</b>
    Quickly save posts and find them later in the Bookmarks list.
  </li>
  <li>
    <b>Follow hashtags:</b>
    See new posts from specific hashtags directly in your home timeline by following them.
  </li>
  <li>
    <b>Answering follow requests:</b>
    Accept or decline follow requests from your notifications or the dedicated Follow requests list.
  </li>
  <li>
    <b>Delete and re-draft:</b>
    The much-loved feature that made editing possible without an actual editing function.
  </li>
</ul>

<p><b>Unreleased official features, available in advance</b></p>

<ul>
  <li><b>Editing posts</b></li>
  <li><b>Integrated updater for GitHub releases</b></li>
</ul>
